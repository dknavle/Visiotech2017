M.G.M's College of Engineering organizes a national technical event VISIOTECH 'Today's Vision, Tomorrow's Technology'. It is a national level program for the aspirants to exhibit their talent and skills through fierce competitions. It intends to provide host of opportunities to different branches’ would be technocrafts with the help of various technical and non technical events. The VISIOTECH will be packed with multiple competitions at different levels enhancing the knowledge and testing capabilities of would be engineers.
