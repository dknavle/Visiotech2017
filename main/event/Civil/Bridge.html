Bridge It


DESCRIPTION:

Right from the beginning of civilisation, Bridges have been an important aspect of transportation. A civil Engineer must have ability of critical thinking, solving different problems, working in a team and finding innovative solutions to day to day problems. This event mainly focuses on developing such abilities among the participants. Through this competition we want to generate sparks in an individual to achieve and conquer the difficulties with some sort of fun. This event will definitely help participants in exploration of new pursuits of math, science and engineering.



EVENT STRUCTURE:

Each team will be provided with popsickle sticks and adhesives and are expected to construct a bridge that would sustain maximum load.

RULES:


  1. The team has to construct the model from provided material only.
  2. No extra material will be provided. The team should use only that material which is provided by the organizers. If it is found that the material used is other than provided by the organisers then the team will be disqualified immediately.
  3. No extra material will be provided. The team should use only that material which is provided by the organizers. If it is found that the material used is other than provided by the organisers then the team will be disqualified immediately.
  4. One team can make only one model for proposed competition.
  5. The decision of the Judges and Event Heads is final and binding to all.
  6. Testing of models will be done according to the serial numbers given to the Particular team at the time of commencement of the event hence no team should request their model tobe tested first.
  7. Students must bring their valid college identity cards while coming to event.
  8. Rules may be changed without prior intimation. Participants are requested to check the website (www.visiotech.in) regularly for updates.
