Traversing


DESCRIPTION:

Traverse surveying is a popular method of surveying. It is that type of survey in which number of connected survey lines from the frame work, directions and lengths of survey lines are measured with the help of an angle measuring instruments.


STRUCTURE:

You will be provided with a problem statement and you have to calculate the area of given site with the help of provided Traversing equipments.


RULES:

  2. All team members should reach the event venue in time.
  3. Any team that is not ready at the time specified will be disqualified immediately.
  4. The decision of the Judges and Event Heads is final and binding to all.
  5. Checking of calculation will be done according to the serial numbers given to the particular team at the time of commencement of the event hence no team should request for their calculation to be checked first.
  6. Team members may be from different colleges can take participate.
  7. Students must bring their valid college identity cards while coming to event.
