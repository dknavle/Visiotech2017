PROJECT PRESENTATION



EVENT DESCRIP TION:

Preparation of demonstrative model showing the concept of civil engineering and general science. New model highlighting new concepts. Working models of existing civil engineering structures. Demonstrative models giving solutions to problems of existing civil engineering structures.



EVENT STRUCTURE:

Participants should bring their respective models to the competition Participants are expected to prepare a report on A4 size paper and present it as a file explain-ing all the concepts and innovations before judges.



RULES:

  1. Participants have to prepare their model on their own as the process of making will be ques-tioned by the judging panel.
  2. Participants have to bring their model on the event day.
  3. Any type of accessory or material will not be provided by the organisers.


JUDGEMENT CRITERIA;

  1. The model should sufficeappropriately to the need and should also be crystal clear with con-cepts, innovations and making.
  2. Explanation of the construction process, working principle and innovations is mandatory.


TEAM AND FEE STRUCTURE:

  1. Team Size: Maximum 3 participants per team.
  2. Registration Fee: Rs 50/- per participant
