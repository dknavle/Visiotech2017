EVENT DESCRIPTION:

Are you a maniac of applications? So here is your chance to show off your software skills on Android. Here the participants will be given a particular task with a theme and all of you have to do is produce a spectacular application and take away the prizes. So come and show your skills.



STRUCTURE:

There will be two rounds:

  1. Round 1: Elimination Round:

The First round consists of Quiz based on Android programming.

  1. Round 2: App Round

In the Second round create an android app and the theme for the app will be given on the spot.

RULE:

  1. Decision taken by judges/event coordinator will be final.
  2. The task should be completed in the allotted time period.
  3. Use of mobile phones during the task is not allowed. If caught doing so, it will lead to immediate disqualification.
  4. Winning scores of first round will decide the eligible participants for the next round.
  5. Rules may be changed without prior intimation. Participants are requested to check the website http://www.visiotech.in regularly for updates.

TEAM AND FEE STRUCTURE:

  1. It is an individual event. Each student has to participate as an individual.
  2. Registration Fee:50/-per participant.
