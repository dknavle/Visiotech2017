ELECTROEUREKA


DESCRIPTION:

Are you interested in electronics devices and components? If yes, here we present ELECTRO EUREKA. “ELECTRO EUREKA” is based on basics of electronics which will test your practical knowledge of electronics and your ability to implement and rectify circuits.


ROUNDS:

ROUND 1:MCQ TEST

  1. There will be 30 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQ’S) based on “BASIC ELECTRONICS”.
  2. Participants should solve the paper in 30 min.
  3. This will be elimination round.

    4. ROUND 2:CIRCUIT DEBUGGING

  4. Two participants will be separated as A & B.
  5. Participant A should come and build the provided circuit in front of judge. Then judges will remove one of the components from the built circuit in the absence of participants.
  6. Participant ‘A’ should tell the description of circuit to participant ‘B’ orally in front of judges within 10 min, then participant ‘B’ will come and debug the circuit.

    7. ROUND 3:ASSEMBLE THE COMPONENTS

  7. In this round both the participants can take part at a time.
  8. The clues will be provided for the components. From the given clues they should identify the components for the circuit.
  9. After identifying components of circuit, we will provide a circuit title which can be build using components from clues.
  10. The group who will build and run the circuit successfully that group will be awarded.


RULES:


  1. Maximum 2 participants will be allowed.
  2. Usage of smart phones, Programmable and Non-programmable calculators and any other electronic/machines will not be allowed within the competition venue.

TEAM AND FEE STRUCTURE:

  1. Team Size : Maximum 2 participants per team.
  2. Registration Fee : Rs 50/- per participant
