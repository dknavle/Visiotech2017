Techno Voltz


DESCRIPTION:

This event will be totally based on “DIGITAL ELECTONICS”. Participants will come to know about logical designing of digital electronics.


ROUNDS:

ROUND 1: MEMORY TEST

  1. This round will be elimination round so the participants have to be careful.
  2. There will be electronic components and different components of circuit arrange on table.
  3. The participants will be asked to observe and memories the components as they will be asked to enlist them in form of “MEMORY GAME” in another hall.

ROUND 2: CIRCUIT BUILDING AND LABELLING

  1. One circuit diagram will be provided to participants.
  2. Both participants will have to run the circuit and show the successful output.
  3. Participants should give proper title to the circuit.
  4. The group who will show the output first will be qualify for the next round.

ROUND 3: SURPRISE ROUND

  1. The judges will provide some mysterious task to participants.
  2. On the basis of winning criteria winner will be decided.

RULES:


  1. Maximum 2 participants will be allowed.
  2. The participant should produce valid ID cards.
  3. This event comprises 3 levels.

TEAM AND FEE STRUCTURE:

Team Size : Maximum 2 participants per team.
Registration Fee : Rs 50/- per participant.
