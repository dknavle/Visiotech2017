VIRTUAL CAMPUS


DESCRIPTION:

"Show your talents and Get Hired to the Corporate." An open opportunity for all undergraduates to get exposed to the corporate process of hiring the undergraduate engineers. A competition which will let you know where you fits in this competitive world of engineers, pursuing for corporate jobs. A platform to calibrate yourself and tune yourself so as to get hired. At MGM's College of Engineering Visio-Tech 2017 we are organizing a prototype event as a competition among the participants like the campus selection process. The Rounds are similar as that of the selection procedure of the corporate companies.viz.
1. Aptitude
2. Group Discussion
3. Personal Interview (Technical).
4. Human Resources.

RULES:

  1. Participants have to appear 1st for aptitude test round. Elimination will take from aptitude test round.
  2. Students cracking aptitude will be allowed to appear for the GD round. Elimination will be on the basis of your performance.
  3. Students sorted through from above two rounds will be appearing for the Personal In-terview round.
  4. Students selected in above rounds will be appearing for HR round where your behaviour and soft skills will be tested.
  5. Winners will be declared on the basis of evaluations from Organizers and Jury.
  6. Organizers have full authority to change the rules without prior intimation.

Note:

  1. Bring 2 Copies of Resume.
  2. Bring 2 Copies of Passport Size Photographs.
  3. Students who will be selected for Personal Interview and HR round must wear formal dress for the same.

FEE STRUCTURE:

  1. Rs 50/- per participant.
