LATHO



EVENT DESCRIPTION:

Latho is a competition on the lathe machine which allows the student to apply their knowledge and skills about basic manufacturing processes. It requires the skill of time management and accuracy in the work. In brief this competition opens up the basic practical ‘world of manufacturing’ for students.



EVENT STRUCTURE:

Round 1: Make a job using machining operations.

RULES:

  1. The Apron is a must.
  2. The total duration of event per batch is 1h.
  3. Participant must report 15 minutes before their allotted batch time.
  4. A drawing of the job will be provided at the time of event.
  5. Tools required for the event will be given only once to the candidates.
  6. Decision of the judges and the event heads will be final and binding to all.
  7. Rules may be changed without prior intimation. Participants are requested.
  8. Check our website http://www.visiotech.in regularly for updates.

  2. Registration Fee: Rs 50/- per team.
