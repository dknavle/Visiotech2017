PROJECT EXHIBITION



EVENT DESCRIPTION:

Have you ever made your thoughts come into existence in front of your eyes? Now it is chance to show your mechanical skills. Here participants bring their mechanical model and take away the prizes. So come and show your mechanical skills.



RULES:

  1. Participants should make hardcopy of their project by maintaining following sequence :
    1. Name of the project ( Title )
    2. Name of the participants
    3. Material used in project
    4. Construction
    5. Working
    6. Application
  2. Submit Project report not exceeding 20 pages.
  3. Display of prototype / Model of the project is compulsory.
  4. IF ANY OTHER REQUIREMENTS NEEDED THEN CONTACT THE COORDINATE MEMBERS BEFORE THE EVENT. NO MEASURE WILL BE TAKEN ON THE DAY OF EVENT.
  5. Participants must contact the event head after the registration.
  6. Winning criteria depend upon your Presentation, appearance, application and simplicity of the project.
  7. Decision taken by judges and organizers will be obligatory to all.The decision of judges will be final and binding to all.
  8. Rules may be changed without prior intimation. Participants are requested to check the website ( www . visiotech.in ) for updates.

TEAM AND FEE STRUCTURE:

  1. Each team can have maximum 4 members.
  2. Rs 50 /- per participant.
